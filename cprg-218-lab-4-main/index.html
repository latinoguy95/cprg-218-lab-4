Origins

Juan Tovar Coffee's origins take place in the Colombian Departments of Quindio, Valle del Cauca, Risaralda, Caldas, Tolima and Huila, where hardworking and humble coffee harvesters put their entire hearts and souls to bring Canadians the best coffee in the entire world. From the most remote mountains in Colombia, and inspired in brands like Juan Valdez Cafe, Cafe Quindio and Oma, Juan Tovar Coffee's staff is commited to bring you a unique, magical and charming experience that will make you want to travel to Colombia just to have some fresh coffee for breakfast.

Learn more!

Born in Ecuador, but lived most of his life in Cali-Colombia, Juan Tovar emigrated from Colombia to Calgary, AB (Canada) for seeking better opportunities and to study a career that allowed him to perform as a journalist (after earning a Bachelor's in Journalism back in Colombia). Seeing coffee as an opportunity to bring his Latin heritage to Canada, Juan started to research about the availabilty of Colombian coffee in Calgary overall and he realized he could create something that would appeal both Colombians (and Latinos overall) and Canadians to make them feel that magical and surreal experience only Colombian coffee could bring.

Colombian coffee is worldiwe known for its aroma and its quality. Juan Tovar Coffee is always committed to make sure that the Magic Realism mood proposed by Liteature Nobel Prize winner Gabriel Garcia Marquez is always present on its coffee, food, and products in general.

Everything starts with our Coffee harversters, who from the Coffeelands (Northern Valle del Cauca, the Departments of Quindio, Risaralda, Caldas, West Tolima and Huila, and Southern Antioquia) get the Coffee Cherry. Coffee is actually made out of the seeds of this fruit, which is roasted next and grinded, and finally ready for consumption. We value the efforts of all the Colombian Coffee harvesters, without their hard labor, commitment and sacrifice, Juan Tovar Coffee wouldn't be possible.
